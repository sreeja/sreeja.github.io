About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science, working on an intersection of programming languages and distributed systems, advised by Marc Shapiro.

Since 2017, I am part of DELYS, a joint group between LIP6 (the computer science laboratory), Sorbonne Université and Inria in Paris, France.

I previously worked with ABB Corporate Research, where I developed a static analysis tool for industrial domain specific languages, from 2014 until 2017. I was working with Infosys as a Software Engineer from 2008 until 2012.
